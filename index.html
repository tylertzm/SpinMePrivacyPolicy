Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

FlickSpinner ("the app") is a fidget spinner app developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

FlickSpinner does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

  • No user account is required
  • No analytics SDKs are used
  • No advertising SDKs are used
  • No crash reporting services are used

On-Device Data Storage

FlickSpinner stores gameplay statistics locally on your device using Apple UserDefaults, including:

  • Daily spin count
  • Monthly spin count
  • Yearly spin count
  • Highest combo

This data stays on your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Device Sensors

FlickSpinner accesses motion sensor data (such as accelerometer/gyroscope data via Core Motion) only to power spinner physics and gameplay interactions. Motion data is processed on-device in real time, is not linked to your identity, is not stored by the app, and is not transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

FlickSpinner does not use third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

FlickSpinner does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Platform Services

If you download FlickSpinner through the App Store, Apple may process certain information under Apple's own privacy practices. This processing is outside FlickSpinner and governed by Apple's policies.

Changes to This Policy

If FlickSpinner's data practices change in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
