Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

SpinMe ("the app") is a fidget spinner app developed by an independent developer. This policy explains how your information is handled.

Data Collection

SpinMe does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No accounts are required. No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are used.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your spin statistics (daily, monthly, yearly counts and best combo) locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

SpinMe does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect data.

Children's Privacy

SpinMe does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly.
